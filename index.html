Welcome to Gem Collector! You have been given the title of Master Gem Collector. At the beginning of each collection, the Geode Extraction Machine (G.E.M.) will extract crystals from geodes and will output a specific weight value. Your mission will be to match G.E.M.'s weight by adding your own crystals to your scale. Before you, are four special crystals, each assigned a hidden weight. If you fail to match the weight, G.E.M.'s collection goes to spoil and you risk being stripped of your new title. However, if you prove yourself worthy you may just gain yourself the title of Gem Master! Be careful though! After each collection, your crystals will have different weights.
